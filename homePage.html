Jinya Ramen Bar - Vegan Red Fire Opal
The Vegan Red Fire Opal Ramen without cilantro

Name:

Jinya Ramen Bar - Vegan Red Fire Opal

Location:

Denver, CO + Other

Rating: 🍜 🍜 🍜 🍜 🍜

This is more bright and refreshing than you would expect from a bowl of ramen. It's a dish that definitely understands the time-tested cooking mantra of salt, fat, acid and heat. Tangy lemon cuts through the otherwise savory and spicy broth. The mushrooms have their signature umami taste, but have been marinated in something sweet, making for a delightful combination. But don’t worry, the sweetness is super subtle and plays well with everything else in the bowl. Bamboo shoots aren’t always my favorite topping, but here they have been sliced so thinly that they turn out crisp and light. They make for some great textural variation.

Overall, Jinya has managed to craft a bowl of ramen that is perfect for the summer!
